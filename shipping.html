Project Delivery.

1. Digital Delivery Only

Since we provide digital services, all final project files (Source code, Designs, Assets) are delivered via email, cloud storage (Google Drive/Dropbox), or GitHub repositories.

2. Timeline

Standard delivery time for UI/UX projects is 7-14 business days. Web development projects may take 21-30 days depending on complexity.

3. Final Assets

Clients will receive high-resolution files and full ownership only after the final payment is cleared.
