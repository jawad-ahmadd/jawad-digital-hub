Refund &
Exchange.

Updated: March 2026

1. Digital Service Nature

Due to the custom and intellectual nature of our digital services (Design & Dev), refunds are handled with strict elite protocols.

2. Eligibility for Refund

Refund requests are only entertained if the project has not yet reached the "Design Approval" milestone.

3. Non-Refundable Items

Domain registrations, hosting fees, and any third-party premium assets purchased for your project are strictly non-refundable.

4. Exchange of Services

Clients can "Exchange" a service for another of equal value (e.g., swapping SEO for Social Media Design) if the work hasn't started.

5. 24-Hour Cancellation

A full refund (minus 5% transaction fee) is available if you cancel within 24 hours of booking any investment plan.

6. Partial Refunds

If a project is cancelled mid-way, we offer a partial refund proportional to the work remaining as per elite hub standards.

7. Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within 7-10 business days via the original payment method.

8. Quality Disputes

If a client is unsatisfied, we offer unlimited revisions (within plan limits) before considering a refund request.

9. Administrative Fees

A standard 15% administrative and setup fee is deducted from all eligible refund amounts.

10. International Client Rules

International transaction fees or currency conversion losses are the responsibility of the client.

11. Force Majeure

Refunds are not issued for delays caused by global internet outages, server host crashes, or natural disasters.

12. Contact for Disputes

For any refund-related queries, email our billing department at jawadriaz2009@gmail.com.
