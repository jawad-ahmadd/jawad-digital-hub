Refund Policy.

1. No Refund Policy

Once the project has commenced and the deposit is paid, **no refunds** will be issued under any circumstances. This covers the creative resources and time allocated to your project.

2. Revisions & Extra Charges

Standard packages include a fixed number of revisions. Any **additional changes** or out-of-scope requests after the project is finalized will incur **extra charges** based on our hourly rate.

3. Cancellation

If a client decides to cancel mid-project, the upfront payment remains non-refundable, and Jawad Digital Hub reserves the right to bill for any additional work completed up to that date.
