1. Introduction & Scope

At Jawad Digital Hub, we are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy outlines how we handle information across our elite digital services, ensuring transparency and security for every global client.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users. This includes:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, and WhatsApp contact details.
  • Business Data: Project briefs, brand assets, and confidential business strategies.

3. Methods of Collection

Data is collected directly when you fill out our "Let's Talk" forms, subscribe to our newsletter, or engage in project consultations via encrypted channels.

4. Use of Information

Your information is used strictly for project execution, improving site performance, and providing personalized digital solutions tailored to your niche market.

5. Data Security Protocols

We implement high-level SSL encryption and secure server environments. No unauthorized personnel can access your confidential files or business logic.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies to analyze traffic patterns and enhance your browsing experience on our platform, helping us maintain our elite digital standards.

7. Third-Party Confidentiality

Jawad Digital Hub strictly follows a "No-Share" policy. We do not sell, trade, or leak your data to third-party marketing agencies or external brokers.

8. Client Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Regardless of your location, you have the right to request access to your data, ask for corrections, or demand complete deletion of your records from our servers at any time.

9. Data Retention Policy

We retain project data only as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations. After project completion, sensitive files can be purged upon request.

10. International Transfers

As a global agency based in Pakistan, your data may be processed on servers located outside your home country, always under strict protection standards.

11. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to modify this policy. Any significant changes will be updated on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
